EduConnect is a passion project by Group 20 – AMAL Coders, built for CPAN-228 at Humber College.

Our Mission

EduConnect bridges the gap between passionate educators and eager learners. We believe quality education should be accessible to everyone, anywhere.

Our platform enables instructors to create rich course content, manage assignments, track student progress, and issue digital certificates upon completion.
